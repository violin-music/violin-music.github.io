## Where to Find Free Scores

- [IMSLP (International Music Score Library Project)](https://imslp.org/) - Public domain classical music scores
- [MuseScore](https://musescore.com/) - Community-created scores
- [CPDL (Choral Public Domain Library)](https://www.cpdl.org/) - For choral works like Handel's Messiah
